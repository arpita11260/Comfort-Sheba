About US

Proper Use Of Antibiotics & Safe Health(PUASH) is a digital platform for peoples of Bangladesh. Here a patient can store their precious data of them, pharmacist can sell medicine without any theft, doctor can give treatment easily, people can not buy extra antibiotics anymore. The platform aims to connect doctors, patients and pharmacists effectively and allowing government to track medicines of whole country from individual pharmacists. That's why every year a ton of money in this medical fields will be saved.
